[[DCAT-AP-20191120]]

Until GeoDCAT-AP version 1.0.1 [[GeoDCAT-AP-20160802]], properties schema:startDate and schema:endDate was used with class dct:PeriodOfTime to specify the beginning and end, respectively, of a temporal interval.

[[VOCAB-DCAT-2]] has defined specific properties for this purpose, namely, dcat:startDate and dcat:endDate, which have been adopted by [[DCAT-AP-20191120]]. Therefore they replace schema:startDate and schema:endDate starting from this version of GeoDCAT-AP.

It is worth noting that dcat:startDate and dcat:endDate are basically equivalent to schema:startDate and schema:endDate.
