Until GeoDCAT-AP version 2.0.0 [[GeoDCAT-AP-20201223]], properties dcat:version, dct:hasVersion, and its inverse dct:isVersionOf were used with class dcat:Dataset to specify, respectively, the version indicator of a dataset and the relationships between a Dataset and its versions.

[[VOCAB-DCAT-3]] has defined specific properties for this purpose, namely, dcat:version, dcat:hasVersion, and dcat:isVersionOf, which have been adopted by [[DCAT-AP-3]]. Therefore they replace, respectively, owl:versionInfo, dct:hasVersion, and dct:isVersionOf starting from this version of GeoDCAT-AP.

It is worth noting that dcat:hasVersion and dcat:isVersionOf are subproperties of dct:hasVersion and dct:isVersionOf, respectively.
